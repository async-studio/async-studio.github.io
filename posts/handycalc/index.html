HandyCalc

HandyCalc is a light calculator app which makes the calculation easier.

Features:

  • Display the input records, so you can make sure your input is correct.
  • Undo the input, if you make a mistake, you can easily undo and redo what you want, even if you have enter the "="
  • The app support iOS, iPad and Mac
